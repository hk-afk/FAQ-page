Frequently Asked Question

1. What is an FAQ page?
An FAQ (frequently asked questions) page answers the most common questions customers and other website visitors have. It provides information related to products and policies, allowing people to source answers independently, without support.
2. Why do you need an FAQ page?
Many customers find your website through search engines. A comprehensive FAQ page is a powerful way to capture organic search traffic. Well-written FAQ answers can appear as featured snippets on Google Search results pages.
